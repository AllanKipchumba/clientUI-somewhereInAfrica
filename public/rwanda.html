Accommodations In Rwanda

Rwanda's diverse landscapes, from national parks to lakeshores, are complemented by a range of accommodation options that cater to various preferences and budgets. Whether you seek luxury, eco-friendly experiences, or budget-friendly stays, Rwanda provides a chance to immerse yourself in its unique culture and natural beauty.

Kigali Serena Hotel

Situated on a picturesque boulevard in Kigali, the luxurious Kigali Serena Hotel boasts a five-star experience. The hotel is designed around a sophisticated granite atrium that highlights the richness of Rwandese cultural art. The ambiance is both laid-back and refined, with notable features including an Executive Lounge and Bar that leads to a spacious sundeck overlooking the recently upgraded swimming pool. Providing a scenic view of the pool as well, the popular Milima Restaurant presents an extensive selection of buffet options throughout the day. For a more relaxed setting, the Sokoni Caf√© offers seating options both indoors and outdoors, alongside the pool.

Ingagi Park View Lodge

Ingagi Park View Lodge provides breathtaking and unobstructed vistas of the Virunga Mountains, the habitat of the endangered Mountain Gorillas. Nestled at the base of the Virunga Volcanoes, we are conveniently located just a short distance from the starting point for gorilla tracking. Renowned for our warm hospitality and attentive service, we take pride in our stunning natural surroundings. Our Africa-inspired lodge is designed with eco-friendliness in mind, constructed using sustainable local materials wherever possible. Each cottage features a private veranda and expansive windows, with some offering stunning panoramic views of the mountains and the flourishing natural landscape.
